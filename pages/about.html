We are Costellae Studios, a humble indie game development team consisting of 2 people currently: Mason Flannery, and Nathan Abney. We hope to over time strengthen our game development knowledge and skills in order to provide you with the best experience possible.

Nathan Abney

Hello, I am Nathan Abney, a co-founder of Costellae Studios. I am currently a sophomore at Miami University majoring in Software Engineering. In middle school, I learned that I like programming through a clunky app on the 3ds titled 'Petit Computer'. It wasn't a very good application, and I wouldn't recommend it to anybody trying to enter programming, but it inspired me to continue programming through high school, where my final project my junior year was to create a video game in javascript about my teacher at the time dodging falling hammers. All of this led me to the decision of becoming a Software Engineering Major. Where I'm at now, I'd love to get Costellae to a level where a younger audience will be inspired by what we do and create.

Mason Flannery
